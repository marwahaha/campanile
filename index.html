Brick by Brick

A Tribute to the Campanile


This icon was completed in 1914 and has been a fixture of the Berkeley campus ever since.

This site is dedicated to the Campanile's 100th year as a symbol of the university and town. Climb the side of the tower - read and contribute memories held within its bricks.
